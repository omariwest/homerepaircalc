Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how HomeRepairCalc may use cookies and similar technologies for functionality, analytics, advertising, and affiliate tracking.

Last updated: May 8, 2026

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They help websites function properly, remember preferences, analyze traffic, and support advertising and affiliate measurement.

1. Types of cookies we may use

  • Essential cookies: Help core site features work properly
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand traffic and improve pages
  • Advertising cookies: Used by ad partners to serve and measure ads
  • Affiliate tracking cookies: Help track referrals to partner websites
  • Preference cookies: May remember basic user choices if used on the site

2. Why we use cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep the site working reliably
  • Measure usage and improve content performance
  • Understand which estimators and repair pages users find most useful
  • Support advertising on the site
  • Measure affiliate link referrals and related performance

3. Third-party cookies

Some cookies may be set by third-party services used on HomeRepairCalc, such as analytics providers, advertising networks, affiliate platforms, hosting tools, or security services.

These third parties may collect information according to their own privacy and cookie policies, which we do not control.

4. Advertising and affiliate cookies

HomeRepairCalc may use advertising or affiliate partners in the future. These partners may use cookies or similar technologies to measure ad performance, track referrals, prevent fraud, or understand whether a user clicked a partner link.

Affiliate or advertising cookies are managed by the relevant third-party providers and may be subject to their own policies.

5. Your cookie choices

Most browsers allow you to block, delete, or limit cookies through browser settings. You can also clear existing cookies from your device.

Please note that disabling some cookies may affect website performance, analytics accuracy, ad personalization, or the way some features display.

6. Related privacy information

For more information on how data may be collected and used on the website, please review our Privacy Policy.

7. Updates to this Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect site changes, advertising setup changes, affiliate programs, analytics tools, or legal updates. The latest version will be posted here with a revised “Last updated” date.

8. Contact

For questions about cookies or site privacy practices, contact support@homerepaircalc.com.

This policy is provided for transparency and website operation purposes. It is not legal advice.
